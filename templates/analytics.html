Methodology Notes

Class Imbalance Handling

Used class_weight="balanced" on applicable models and lowered the classification threshold to 0.30 to maximize Recall and minimize false negatives in critical diagnoses.

Hyperparameter Tuning

Models were configured with tuned hyperparameters: max_depth, n_estimators, learning_rate, C, and gamma — prioritizing Recall over Precision for clinical safety.

Features Used

Gender, Age, BMI, Avg Glucose, Hypertension, Heart Disease, Ever Married, Work Type, Residence Type, Smoking Status.

Evaluation Priority

Recall is prioritized over Precision. A false negative (missed stroke) is far more costly than a false positive in clinical settings.
